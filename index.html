Dr. Guruprasad
Founder & Lead

Dr. Guruprasad has completed his UG studies from GMIT Davanagere and PG studies from JNNCE Shimoga. he has received his Phd from SJCE, Mysore under VTU. His research interests are manufacturing of micro sensors for customised applications. he has published sci journals on the the design and fabrication of mems cantilever sensors for the detection of VOCs. he also extended his work fo the carbon dioxide sensors using MEMS cantilvers under INUP IISc, Bangalore. Presently he is working as Senior Assistant Professor in the department of ECE, Alvas institute of engineering and technology, Moodbidri. Along with teaching with his interest in entrepreneurial ideas the esteemed organisation has given opportunity to start the microembeed product based start up V sense Technologies under the patronage of AEF.
